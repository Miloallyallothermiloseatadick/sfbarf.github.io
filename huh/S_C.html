SHOPPING CART OUTRAGE!

LOCAL GENIUS OR CRAZY PERSON?

Why are shopping carts increasing in size? Is this the end of small shopping carts as we know it?.

I interviewed a Local Complainer about the increase of shopping carts at the Berkeley Bowl. Many people have complained about the increasing shopping cart size. Its become a huge problem in the world of light shoppers.

People who buy fewer groceries would usually rather have an easily maneuverable cart so they can get the few groceries they came there to buy. This problem is driving people to desperate measures and this is the story of one such desperate shopper who took matters into her own hands.

Local Complainer :
Every time I go to Berkeley Bowl I go to customer service and ask them to get smaller shopping carts. The first time I even asked about it the guy said, "Have bigger shopping carts? Yeah, I know." This shows that more people have complained about the abominable size of the shopping cart. The last time I went there the guy said, "We're not getting smaller shopping carts because the big ones make you want to buy more food." Local genius got discouraged so I realized I was on my own and stopped complaining.

Fast forward to Friday night. It was dark and stormy and I got off work pretty late. I drove to my friends house and I was going to park. It was trash night so there were trash cans out. When I pulled up I noticed that what I had before thought was a trash can was one of those half sized shopping carts that I had wanted so badly! I couldn't believe my luck. I was sure it was going to be broken but it was not. I was soaked by then. I found out it worked perfectly so I put it in the backseat of my 1991 Volkswagen cabriolet and took it home.
