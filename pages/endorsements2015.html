The Method

From August 19th to September 8th members of the SFBA Renters' Federation debated the ballot measures and voted on their endorsements online. See the online form here. Each ballot measure could be scored between 0 (hate) and 5 (love it) or voters could pick "No Opinon/ PAC should not endorse."

While voting was open, members could change our votes as often as we liked. If 51% of respondents vote "No Opinion ... " on a ballot proposition, the PAC did not endorse. If the average score is between 2 & 3 (and maybe wider, maybe between 1.5 and 3.5) the PAC did not endorse. By the time of closing, 79 members voted. Final averages are diplayed to the right.
